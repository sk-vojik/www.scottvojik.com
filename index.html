CryptAuto.net

CryptAuto.net is a point of sale payment processing platform that enables car dealerships to accept crypto payments without the risk and hassle of crypto custody. CryptAuto leverages Stripe's Connect and Crypto Products. This allows dealers to easily link their bank info for where they would like to receive payments, and generate crypto invoices for their customers in seconds. Customers may then pay with any cryptocurrency that Stripe supports.. Payments are settled in USD and sent to the dealer's bank account, all entirely automated.

PairPlay.gg

PairPlay.gg is a peer-to-peer marketplace that enables gamers and content creators to connect with their fans through paid gaming sessions. PairPlay.gg integrates Stripe's Connect platform for easy payments and payouts, with a custom calendar and booking system to allow users to easily schedule, request, and pay for gaming sessions.

FantasyGambl.com (Coming Soon)

FantasyGambl.com is a fantasy sports platform that mixes the best parts of fantasy sports with sports betting. Instead of wagering real money, users select bets, and winners earn points for choosing correctly. Users compete against other players to see who can win the most units during a FantasyGambl season!
