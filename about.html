About

I am currently a senior software engineer at Cox Automotive, working on Autotrader, and specifically the Private Seller Exchange product. In my free time, I enjoy indie-hacking and creating businesses, racing cars, and being outdoors. I'm a self-motivated, curious individual with a passion for technology, finance (especially crypto), and developing software. I have experience working with small teams in intense startup environments, as well as being part of a 1000+ person engineering organization.
